Community Guidelines

These rules keep ConfessHub a safe, supportive space for everyone. Please read them before posting.

ConfessHub is built on trust. These are the things we celebrate and encourage in our community.

1
Be honest

Share your genuine feelings and thoughts. Authenticity is what makes this community valuable.

2
Be kind in comments

Respond with empathy. Everyone here is being vulnerable — treat them the way you'd want to be treated.

3
Use categories wisely

Tag your confession correctly so others with similar experiences can find it and connect with you.

4
Support others

Leave supportive comments. A kind word can make a huge difference to someone going through a hard time.

5
Keep it real

Share actual thoughts and experiences. This isn't a place for fictional stories or creative writing.

6
Reach out for help

If you or someone in the comments needs real help, encourage them to seek professional support. We care about mental health.

Violating these rules will result in your content being removed. Repeated violations may result in your IP being blocked from posting.

1
No hate speech

Content targeting people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexuality, or disability will be removed immediately.

2
No personal information

Never share full names, addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying information about real people.

3
No bullying or harassment

Using this platform to target, mock, or harass specific individuals is strictly prohibited.

4
No explicit content

Graphic sexual content, graphic violence, or content involving minors is absolutely not permitted.

5
No spam or self-promotion

Don't use ConfessHub to advertise products, services, or to post repetitive content.

6
No illegal content

Confessions that describe, promote, or incite illegal activities will be removed and may be reported.

Your privacy is our #1 priority. Here is exactly what we do and don't collect.

What we do NOT collect
  • Your name, email, or any account details
  • Your IP address or device fingerprint
  • Your location or browser history
  • Any personally identifiable information
What we do store
  • The text of your confession
  • The category you selected
  • Timestamp of when it was posted
  • Anonymous interaction counts (likes, comments)
Local storage

Your likes, comments, and confessions are stored in your own browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you post a confession publicly.

Cookies

We only use cookies that are strictly necessary for the site to function. We do not use any tracking or advertising cookies.

Is my confession really anonymous?
Yes, completely. We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any other identifying information. Once your confession is submitted, there is no way for us or anyone else to trace it back to you.
Can I delete my confession after posting?
Because confessions are anonymous, we cannot verify ownership, so there is no self-delete feature for published confessions. However, you can report content that violates community rules for moderator review.
How are comments moderated?
Comments go through an automated filter for obvious policy violations. The community can also flag inappropriate comments for human review. We aim to review flagged content within 24 hours.
What if someone is in danger?
If you see a post suggesting someone is in immediate danger, please use the report button and select "Safety concern." Our team reviews these urgently. If you or someone you know is in crisis, please contact a crisis helpline in your country immediately.
Do I need to create an account?
No. ConfessHub requires zero account creation. You can post confessions, leave comments, and interact with content entirely without signing up or logging in.
Can I share ConfessHub posts on social media?
Yes, you can share the link to any confession. However, please be mindful that even though authors are anonymous, sharing confessions widely could still feel violating to the person who posted. Use good judgment.
